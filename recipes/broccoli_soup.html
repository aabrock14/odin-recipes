Broccoli Soup

Description

This is the best broccoli soup out there.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 bag (16 ounces) frozen broccoli florets, thawed
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 8 ounces low fat shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Put a pot on the stove over medium heat.
  2. Warm oil and fry the diced onion.
  3. Add butter to the pot and melt.
  4. Whisk in the flour until a roux develops.
  5. Whisk in the chicken broth and cream.
  6. Add bay leaf, and turn up heat to high; bring soup to a boil.
  7. Add the broccoli florets and all spices, and cook over medium heat for 20 minutes.
  8. Pur√©e as desired with immersion blender.
  9. Continue to cook over low heat and add the grated cheese; stir until melted and completely combined.
  10. Remove from heat.
  11. Taste for seasoning and adjust accordingly.
  12. Serve.
